Thank you, beautiful

Your new brows are an investment — and the next 5 days decide how gorgeously they heal. This is your guide, exactly as Amii teaches it. Follow it, and your color will settle soft, even, and true.

Your daily ritual · Days 1–5

1

Gently wash or wipe your brows 3–5 times a day with cool water and a gentle, fragrance-free soap made for sensitive skin. Soft fingertips only — no scrubbing.

2

After each wash, apply the cream provided at your appointment across both brows.

3

Repeat this ritual for the next 5 days. Consistency is what heals your color soft, even, and true.

Your wash

Gentle, fragrance-free soap for sensitive skin

Cool water only. Nothing scented, nothing harsh.

Your healing cream

The cream provided at your appointment

Apply after every wash — this is the only product that touches your brows while they heal.

While you heal — the don'ts

  • No sweating — and that means none. Skip the gym and anything that works up heat
  • No pool water, beach or river water, and no steamy hot showers
  • No direct sunlight on your brows
  • No makeup, creams, or any other products on the brow area — only your provided cream
  • No rubbing, scratching, or picking at your brows

The 4-week rule

✦

No procedures or applications to the brows until your 4-week healing process is complete. Your skin needs the full four weeks to settle the color — protect it, and it will reward you.

Healing is part of the beauty. Trust it. — Amii
