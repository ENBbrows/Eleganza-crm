About Your Microblading Treatment
This is a semi-permanent manual technique — pigment is placed in the surface layer of the skin in fine, hair-like strokes matched to your natural color. Color is noticeably darker for the first 5-7 days, then fades 40-50% as it settles. Full healing takes about 28 days, and a follow-up touch-up afterward is a normal, expected part of the process. Results typically last 10-12 months (as little as 6 months on oilier skin).
In the 30 days before your appointment, avoid: botox/fillers, chemical peels or fruit/milk acid treatments, laser treatments, and strong sun exposure or tanning.
